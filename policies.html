We offer Free Standard Shipping on orders over $59! Please allow between 3-7 business days for your order to arrive. Return Policies:

At Durham House of Pets, your satisfaction is very important to us. We know pet parents are busy, so we've made our return policies easy and convenient. You can choose return an item in store or you can mail it to: 48 Oyster River Rd, Durham, NH,03824. If you're not satisfied with your DHP purchase, you can return the product for the purchase price. Product must be in its original packaging. Simply bring the product in its original packaging plus the packing slip or the shipping confirmation email as proof of purchase. We provide 30 days’ return policy, and the product should return in its original packaging and in new or salable condition. If you order our product online, we will return you only the product price, not include shipping fee. In addition, gift cards are non-returnable item. Let our employee know if you have any question. Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment。
